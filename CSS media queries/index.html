What is bubble tea?

Bubble tea is a tea based drink that goes by various different names. It is a drink high on sugar and calories, often paired with chewy tapioca balls

Alternative names

  1. Boba
  2. Boba tea
  3. Boba nai cha
  4. Bubble tea
  5. Tapioca tea
  6. Pearl milk tea
